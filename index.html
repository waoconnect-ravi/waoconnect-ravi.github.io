Why O2X?

Streamline Data

Import & Export.

Import or export a wide array of data such as invoices, bills, credit notes, payments & refunds, tracking categories, Chart of Accounts & tax codes, between Odoo and Xero, promoting seamless data management.

Eradicate Manual

Entry Hassles.

No more manual data entry! The O2X integration facilitates a smooth, two-way data flow, saving time, minimising errors, and ensuring the accuracy of financial information.

Real-time Data

Harmony.

Achieve real-time synchronisation for crucial data including stock journals, stock adjustment journals, Cost Of Goods Sold, invoices, credit notes and vendor bills through seamless integration with Xero.

Frequency for

Each Process.

From syncing contacts to invoices, payments, and more, steer your ship and program the system to run syncs more frequently or less frequently as per your requirement.

eCommerce

Transaction Data Flow.

Receive batched e-commerce transactions to reduce the number of transactions flowing into the system to avoid system clogs.

Sync Error

Notifications.

Get notified via email when errors prevent data syncs between the two systems. While successful syncs will appear in green, unsuccessful syncs will appear in red.

Increased

Departmental Collaboration.

Bridge departmental gaps and facilitate the seamless flow of information, fostering better collaboration across your organization.
